# Domain Dispute Resolution

## UDRP (Uniform Domain-Name Dispute-Resolution Policy)

### Filing Requirements (Complainant must prove ALL three)

1. **Identical or confusingly similar** — Domain is identical or confusingly similar to a trademark in which the complainant has rights
2. **No rights or legitimate interests** — The domain holder has no rights or legitimate interests in the domain
3. **Bad faith registration and use** — The domain was registered and is being used in bad faith

### Bad Faith Indicators

- Registered to sell to trademark owner at inflated price
- Pattern of registering domains to prevent trademark owners from using them
- Registered to disrupt a competitor's business
- Using the domain to attract users by creating confusion with the trademark

### Legitimate Interest Defenses

- Using the domain for a bona fide offering of goods/services before the dispute
- Commonly known by the domain name (even without trademark)
- Legitimate noncommercial or fair use with no intent to mislead

### UDRP Process

| Step | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| Complaint filed with UDRP provider | Day 0 |
| Provider verifies complaint | 3–5 days |
| Respondent notified | Day ~5 |
| Response deadline | 20 days from notification |
| Panel appointed | ~5 days after response deadline |
| Decision issued | 14 days after panel appointment |
| Implementation (if transfer/cancellation) | 10 business days after decision |

**Total timeline:** ~45–60 days

### UDRP Providers

| Provider | Abbreviation |
|----------|-------------|
| World Intellectual Property Organization | WIPO |
| National Arbitration Forum | Forum |
| Asian Domain Name Dispute Resolution Centre | ADNDRC |
| Czech Arbitration Court | CAC |
| Arab Center for Dispute Resolution | ACDR |

### Outcomes

- **Transfer** — Domain transferred to complainant
- **Cancellation** — Domain registration cancelled
- **Denial** — Complaint denied, domain stays with respondent

## URS (Uniform Rapid Suspension)

Faster, cheaper alternative for clear-cut infringement cases.

| Aspect | URS | UDRP |
|--------|-----|------|
| **Standard of proof** | Clear and convincing | Preponderance |
| **Cost** | ~$375–$500 | ~$1,500–$4,000+ |
| **Timeline** | ~18–21 days | ~45–60 days |
| **Outcome** | Suspension only (no transfer) | Transfer or cancellation |
| **Applicability** | New gTLDs only | All gTLDs |
| **Duration** | Suspended for remainder of registration | Permanent |
